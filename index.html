WELCOME

I'm Lelinah and I want to welcome you to my blog.
What can you learn in 30 days? — The basic idea of this blog is to give you an inside into my monthly Learning Missions. At the beginning of each month, I will choose a new skill to learn. In regular posts, you can watch me struggle, curse, and fail, but hopefully, also grow with each challenge. Besides that, I will also explain and list, which methods, tools, websites, and other things I used to improve the chosen skills. So every post will be part of a series of posts, belonging to a bigger Mission. You can find all of these Missions by clicking the "How it's going" button in the right top corner. On the "How it started" side you will find out more about my motivation and my hopes and plans for this blog. If you are interested in what I am working on right now, go check out my newest posts below!
